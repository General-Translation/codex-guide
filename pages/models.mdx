# Models

We currently provide three large language models (LLMs) for performing translations:

1. **claude-3-opus** from [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/)
1. **gpt-4** from [OpenAI](https://openai.com/)
1. **gemini-pro** from [Google](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/#introduction)

Each model supports a unique set of translation languages, so always make sure to use the one that best suits your translation needs.

### claude-3-opus

The most performant model, Anthropic's **claude-3-opus** delivers the highest quality translation in 27 languages. Due to the cost of running **claude-3-opus**, free users are limited to 250 tokens of translated text per day. You can unlock unlimited access to this model by subscribing to [Codex Alpha](./alpha.mdx).
