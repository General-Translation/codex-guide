# Audio

Listen to your content or translation with the **Audio** feature.

### How to Use Audio

1. **Locating the Audio Button**: You'll find the **Audio** button in both the input and translation area toolbars.

1. **Activating the Audio**: Click on the **Audio** button in the toolbar of the text you wish to hear.
