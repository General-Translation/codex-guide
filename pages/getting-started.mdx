# Getting Started

Welcome to your guide on getting started with Codex. This page will cover some key concepts that are important to understand before using Codex.

### Tokens

Tokens correspond to words, subwords, and characters. They are the building blocks of AI language model input and output. There is currently a limit of 250 tokens per page, roughly equal to 200 words. You can check the number of tokens used by your translation in the sidebar.

### Translation


Codex is intuitive and automatically recognizes the language of your input, but you can specify the language for accuracy. After choosing your translation language and model, just click the **Translate** button to see your text in another language. The sidebar provides detailed statistics - character count, word count, and token usage for both your original text and its translation, offering all the information you need to streamline your translation process.

### Resetting

The **Reset** button clears all the input and translation content for the current [page](https://guide.codex.ai/tools/pages) you're working on. This feature is particularly useful when starting a new translation task or when you need to clear the current content to make room for new text without affecting other pages you may be working with.
